About

GirlOnEarth. is a local female group passionate about living our lives to the fullest. We focus on personal growth, we believe everyone should be constantly working on themselves to fully enjoy life. We gather once a week to share ideas and activities to connect with people and ourselves. The only limits in your life are those that you set yourself. If you are passionate about achieving your highest potential and living your best life, this is THE place for you! A journey of a thousands miles begins with a single step! Let's begin this journey together, girl!
